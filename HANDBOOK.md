# Open Mindi — Player Handbook 🃏

A quick, friendly guide to playing **Open Mindi**. For the full rulebook and the
developer docs, see [`README.md`](./README.md).

---

## 🎯 The goal

Capture as many **tens** (called **mindis**) as you can. The side with the most
captured tens wins the round. Win **all** the tens for a **whitewash** 🏆.

---

## ▶️ How to play

1. On the **home screen**, pick:
   - **Game mode** — *Open Mindi* or *Double Mindi*
   - **Players** — 2–6 (4 and 6 play in **teams**: you + partner vs the others)
   - **Bot difficulty** — *Learner* or *Expert*
   - **Bet** — how many coins to stake on the round
2. Tap **▶ PLAY**.
3. Each player has a few face-down **piles** (only the **top card shows**) plus a
   small **private hand** at the bottom — those are **your cards**.
4. On your turn, **tap any highlighted card** (hand or a pile top) to play it.
   - You **must follow the led suit** if you can.
   - The badge on a pile shows how many cards are still hidden under it.
5. Highest card of the led suit wins the trick — unless someone plays a **trump**
   (shown at the top of the screen), in which case the highest trump wins.
6. Win tricks that contain **tens** to capture mindis. Most mindis wins the round!

---

## 🪙 Coins & bets

- You start with a coin balance shown top-right; tap **+** for more options.
- Claim your **🎁 daily bonus** from the pill next to your avatar.
- Win a round to take the **prize fund**; lose and you forfeit your bet.

---

## 📱 Install it as an app (no browser bar)

Playing in a browser shows the address bar and eats screen space. Install the
game to play **full-screen, like a real app**:

- **Android (Chrome):** tap the **⤓ Install App** button on the home screen, or
  use the browser menu → *Install app / Add to Home screen*.
- **iPhone (Safari):** tap **Share** → **Add to Home Screen**.
- **Desktop (Chrome/Edge):** click the install icon in the address bar.

Once installed it opens full-screen and works **offline**.

---

## 💾 Run it on your PC (single file)

The **`download/`** folder contains one self-contained file —
**`Open-Mindi.html`**. Download it and **double-click it** to play in any modern
browser: no internet, no install, nothing else needed. The whole game (code,
**real card art and music**) is baked into that single file.

---

## 💡 Tips

- Hold high trumps to snatch tricks that contain tens.
- Watch the visible pile tops — you can often tell what opponents are forced to play.
- In team games, let your partner take the ten if they're already winning the trick.

Have fun, and go collect those mindis! 🎴
